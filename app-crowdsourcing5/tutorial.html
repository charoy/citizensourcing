Picture selection

Hi! In this project, you will have to decide which one of two photos depict the best a catastrophic situation

The application is really simple. It loads a photo and asks you this question: Select the best picture to represent the catastrophic situation at that location?

You will have 2 possible answers:

  • Picture 1, or
  • Picture 2

All you have to do is to click in one of the two possible answers and you will be done. You can quit the exercise whenever you want
